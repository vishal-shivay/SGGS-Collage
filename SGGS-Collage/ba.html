Why Choose B.A / B.A (Hons.) at S.G.G.S. Khalsa College Mahilpur?

B.A / B.A (Hons.) equips students with analytical, research, and communication skills in subjects like History, Political Science, Literature, Computer Science, Economics, Mathematics, Physical Education. The program emphasizes critical thinking, cultural awareness, and practical knowledge through projects and assignments.

  • Comprehensive curriculum covering Humanities & Social Sciences
  • Research Projects & Practical Assignments
  • Skill development in communication and analysis
  • Opportunities for higher studies & competitive exams

B.A. / B.A. (Hons.) – Overview

The 3/4 year undergraduate program (6/8 semesters) focuses on Humanities and Social Sciences disciplines, developing critical thinking, research, and academic skills. It prepares students for careers in education, civil services, research, and cultural organizations.

Career Opportunities

Graduates can pursue roles in Teaching, Research, Journalism, Civil Services, Cultural Organizations, or higher studies like M.A., Ph.D., or competitive exams.

Program Outcomes
  • In-depth knowledge of chosen subjects
  • Enhanced research and analytical skills
  • Effective communication and writing skills
  • Preparation for higher studies and professional careers
