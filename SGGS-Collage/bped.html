B.P.E.d – Overview

B.P.Ed. is a 2-year professional program designed for graduates interested in Physical Education, Sports Science, Fitness Training, and Coaching.

Why Choose B.P.Ed.?

  • Hands-on exposure to sports science, fitness assessment, and training programs
  • Develop leadership, teamwork, and coaching skills
  • Opportunities for internships, workshops, and field work
  • Prepares for higher studies like M.P.Ed, coaching certifications, or sports administration

Career Opportunities

Graduates can work as Physical Education Teachers, Fitness Trainers, Sports Coaches, Wellness Consultants, Sports Administrators, or pursue higher studies.

Admission

Admissions for B.P.Ed. are open! Eligible students (Graduates in any discipline) can apply online or offline.

Course Duration & Eligibility

The B.P.Ed. program is a 2-year course divided into 4 semesters.
