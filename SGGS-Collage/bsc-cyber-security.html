Programme Outcomes

What You Will Achieve

Skills & Competencies Gained

01

Develop strong networking and operating system fundamentals essential for understanding and defending cybersecurity threats.

02

Gain hands-on ethical hacking skills using Kali Linux, Metasploit, Burp Suite, Nmap and other industry-standard penetration testing tools.

03

Understand and implement cryptographic algorithms, public key infrastructure, digital signatures and data protection techniques.

04

Perform digital forensics and incident response — collecting, preserving and analysing digital evidence for cybercrime investigations.

05

Participate in Capture The Flag (CTF) competitions, hackathons, and real-world vulnerability disclosure programs.

06

Prepare for globally recognized certifications like CEH, CompTIA Security+, OSCP, CISSP, and government sector cybersecurity exams.

07

Build understanding of cyber law, IT Act, compliance frameworks (ISO 27001, NIST, GDPR) and ethical responsibilities of security professionals.

08

Enhance analytical thinking, team-based incident response and communication skills needed in Security Operations Centers (SOC).
