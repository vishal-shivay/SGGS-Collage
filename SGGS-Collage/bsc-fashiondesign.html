Why Choose B.Sc. Fashion Designing at S.G.G.S. Khalsa College Mahilpur?

B.Sc. Fashion Designing is a professional undergraduate program focusing on creativity, fashion illustration, garment construction, and textile science. The course emphasizes practical workshops, portfolio development, and industry exposure.

  • Modern Fashion & Textile Labs
  • Hands-on Garment Designing Workshops
  • Portfolio Development for Professional Growth
  • Exposure to Fashion Industry Trends
  • Opportunities in Apparel, Fashion Technology, and Entrepreneurship
  • Highly Qualified & Experienced Faculty

B.Sc. Fashion Designing – Overview

This 3/4 year program (6/8 semesters) equips students with knowledge of fashion design, textile studies, garment construction, CAD, and trend forecasting. It prepares students for creative, technical, and managerial roles in fashion industry.

Why Choose B.Sc. Fashion Designing?

  • Hands-on experience in modern Fashion & Textile labs.
  • Develops skills in pattern making, garment construction, and digital designing tools.
  • Opportunities for internships, fashion shows, and exhibitions.
  • Prepares for higher studies in Fashion Technology, MBA (Fashion), or Entrepreneurship.

Career Opportunities

Graduates can work as Fashion Designers, Textile Designers, CAD Designers, Apparel Technologists, Merchandisers, Entrepreneurs, or Fashion Stylists.

Admission

Admissions for B.Sc. Fashion Designing are open! Eligible students (10+2 with Science/Arts or equivalent) can apply online or offline. Secure your seat and start your creative journey in fashion and design.
