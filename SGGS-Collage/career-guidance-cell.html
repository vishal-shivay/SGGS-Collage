About the Cell

Career Guidance and Counselling Cell

S.G.G.S. Khalsa College, Mahilpur

The Career Guidance and Counselling Cell plays a significant role in shaping the future career of students by guiding and mentoring them to opt for the right education path as per their skills, interests and competency. The students are trained to be employable in various private and government sectors and to become entrepreneurs who can foster job opportunities in today's competitive world.

To achieve its aforesaid aims, this cell conducts various workshops, seminars and expert lectures for which eminent persons from academia and industry are invited. Furthermore, the Career Guidance and Counselling Cell provides commendable services in the areas of campus placement, mock interviews, skill development and various hands-on training programs.

Our Support Areas

How We Help Our Students

  • 1Right Education Path: Counselling students to choose the most suitable stream and course based on personal aptitude, interests, and long-term career goals.
  • 2Industry Exposure: Inviting eminent professionals from academia and industry to interact directly with students through expert lectures, workshops, and seminars.
  • 3Campus Placement Assistance: Coordinating with companies and organizations for placement drives, ensuring maximum employment opportunities for graduating students.
  • 4Mock Interview Preparation: Conducting simulated interview sessions to prepare students for real-world recruitment processes in both government and private sectors.
  • 5Skill Enhancement Training: Providing hands-on training programs that improve communication, technical, and professional skills essential for today's job market.
  • 6Entrepreneurship Motivation: Encouraging and training students to become entrepreneurs who can foster job opportunities for others in today's competitive world.
  • 7Government Sector Guidance: Providing complete information about competitive examinations, government job opportunities, and preparation strategies.
  • 8Private Sector Opportunities: Helping students identify, apply for, and succeed in opportunities within leading private sector companies and organizations.
