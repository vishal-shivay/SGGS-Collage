Frequently Asked Questions

What is the eligibility for B.Voc Horticulture Sciences?
10+2 / Intermediate in any stream from a recognized board. Students with Agriculture or Biology background are preferred but not mandatory.
Are field visits and internships included?
Yes — field visits, nursery internships, farm training and industry exposure are core parts of the program across all semesters.
Can I start my own business after B.Voc Horticulture?
Yes — the program includes agri-entrepreneurship training, enabling graduates to set up their own nurseries, farms, or landscaping ventures.
What government job opportunities are available?
Graduates can apply for Horticulture Officer, Agricultural Extension Officer, and technical posts in state and central government horticulture departments.
Can I pursue higher studies after this program?
Yes — graduates can pursue B.Sc. (Hons.) Agriculture, M.Sc. Horticulture, or related PG programs at agricultural universities.
