Frequently Asked Questions

What is the eligibility for B.B.A. (BFSI)?
10+2 from any recognized board. Students from any stream (Commerce, Science, Arts) are eligible to apply.
What is the duration of the program?
3 years (6 semesters) full-time.
Are internships and certifications included?
Yes — industry internships and BFSI-related certifications are an integral part of the program, especially in semesters 5 and 6.
What industries can I work in after this program?
Graduates can work in banks, insurance companies, NBFCs, mutual fund firms, stock broking companies, and FinTech startups.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the eligibility for B.B.A. (Honours)?
10+2 from any recognized board with a minimum percentage as per university norms. Students from Commerce, Science and Arts streams are eligible.
How is B.B.A. (Honours) different from a regular BBA?
B.B.A. (Honours) includes an additional research component, advanced specialization papers and a dissertation, making it more rigorous and better suited for students aiming for MBA, PhD or top corporate roles.
What is the duration of the program?
3 years (6 semesters) full-time, with an optional 4th year for Honours research under NEP 2020 guidelines.
Are internships included?
Yes — internships with industry partners are mandatory and are included in semesters 5 and 6.
