Frequently Asked Questions

What is the eligibility criteria?
10+2 / Intermediate from a recognized board with physical fitness clearance.
How long is the B.P.Ed. program?
The program is 3 years, divided into 6 semesters.
Are there internships included?
Yes, internships and practical coaching sessions are included in semesters 5 & 6.
Can I pursue higher studies after B.P.Ed.?
Yes, you can pursue M.P.Ed., MBA in Sports Management, or research roles in physical education.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the eligibility criteria for D.P.Ed.?
Candidates should have completed 10+2 / Intermediate in any stream with minimum 50% marks from a recognized board.
How long is the D.P.Ed. program?
The D.P.Ed. is a 2-year full-time diploma program with intensive practical and theoretical training.
Is practical training included in D.P.Ed.?
Yes, intensive practical sessions in sports, fitness, and coaching are mandatory throughout the program.
Can I pursue teaching after D.P.Ed.?
Yes, D.P.Ed. graduates are eligible for teaching physical education in schools and academies.
Can I upgrade to B.P.Ed. after completing D.P.Ed.?
Yes, D.P.Ed. holders can pursue B.P.Ed. for a full undergraduate degree and further advance to M.P.Ed. for postgraduate studies.
