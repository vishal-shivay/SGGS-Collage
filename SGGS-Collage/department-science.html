M.Sc. Computer Science — Overview

Program Overview

M.Sc. Computer Science is a 2-year postgraduate program (4 semesters, NEP-2020 based) designed for students interested in Advanced Programming, Data Science, Artificial Intelligence and Software Development.

Program Highlights

  • Hands-on exposure to programming, databases and software tools
  • Develop analytical and problem-solving skills for the IT industry
  • Opportunities for research projects, internships and competitive exams
  • Prepares for higher studies like Ph.D., M.Tech or IT certifications
  • Industry-aligned NEP-2020 based curriculum

Duration & Eligibility

Duration: 2 Years (4 Semesters)  |  Eligibility: B.Sc. CS / IT / Maths / CS with minimum 45% marks

Program Outcomes

  • Develop advanced programming, data analysis and software development skills
  • Hands-on experience with AI, ML, Big Data and Cloud tools
  • Ability to design and implement IT solutions for real-world problems
  • Preparation for internships, research and competitive exams
  • Build leadership, communication and teamwork skills in tech projects
