Frequently Asked Questions

Who is eligible for B.Sc.?
Students who have passed 10+2. Science stream (PCB/PCM) is generally preferred for most combinations.
Can I do M.Sc. after B.Sc.?
Yes — B.Sc. is the standard undergraduate path to M.Sc. and other postgraduate courses.
Are internships part of the program?
Yes — project work, internships and field visits are included to provide practical exposure.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the eligibility for M.Sc.?
A B.Sc. degree in the relevant subject with minimum 45% marks (or as per college/university policy). Eligibility may vary by specialization.
Can I do M.Sc. in a different subject than my B.Sc. major?
In some cases yes — for example, B.Sc. Mathematics students may join M.Sc. Computer Science. Check with the admissions office for subject-specific rules.
Is research project compulsory?
Yes — all M.Sc. programs include a research dissertation and viva in the final semester.
Are scholarships available?
Yes — merit and need-based scholarships are offered. See the admissions office for current government and college-level schemes.
