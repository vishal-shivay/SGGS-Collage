Facilities

World-class infrastructure for learning, growth, and student support.

GADHRI BABA HARJAP SINGH JI CONVENTION HALL

The college has a modern ICT-enabled Convention Hall used for seminars, workshops, guest lectures, annual functions, orientation programs, and college celebrations. It is equipped with a projector, sound system, and comfortable seating arrangement. The hall can accommodate a large audience for academic and cultural events. It provides an ideal environment for interactive learning and professional gatherings. Regular maintenance ensures a clean, technologically advanced, and welcoming space.

SANT HARI SINGH KAHARPURI MEMORIAL LIBRARY

The library is one of the strongest academic resources of the college, containing thousands of books, reference materials, newspapers, magazines, and journals. A spacious reading hall is available for students and faculty to study and conduct research. Digital resources and e-books are accessible to support modern learning methods. It also provides quiet spaces for group discussions and project work. The library is regularly updated to include new academic and research publications.

BUS FACILITY FOR GIRL STUDENTS

The college provides its own transport facility for girl students to ensure safe and comfortable travel from nearby areas. The bus service helps students maintain regular attendance and reach the campus on time. Drivers and staff are trained to ensure punctuality and safety. Multiple routes are planned to cover important areas around the college. This facility supports students' convenience and encourages participation in campus activities.

CO-OPERATIVE MESS / CANTEEN

The college has a well-maintained mess and canteen providing hygienic and affordable food. It offers tea, snacks, and meals for students and staff throughout the day. The seating area provides a comfortable space to relax and socialize. Healthy menu options are available to promote overall wellness among students. The facility is supervised regularly to maintain cleanliness and quality service.

STUDIES CENTRES IN COLLEGE

The college encourages research and academic learning through study centres and academic clubs. Students participate in discussions, presentations, and departmental activities that support skill development. Centers provide access to resources for project work, research, and competitions. Faculty mentors guide students to enhance their learning and practical knowledge. The environment fosters intellectual curiosity, collaboration, and academic excellence.

TEACHERS' FLATS

The college provides accommodation facilities for teachers to support faculty convenience and smooth academic functioning. This helps in the availability of faculty for student guidance and campus activities. Flats are well-furnished and maintained to provide a comfortable living space. Proximity to classrooms allows teachers to participate actively in events and mentoring. It strengthens the bond between students and faculty, enhancing the learning environment.
