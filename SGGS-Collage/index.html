Principal's Message

A Warm Welcome from
the Principal's Desk

"

"Education is not the learning of facts, but the training of the mind to think." – Albert Einstein

As we stand on the threshold of the new academic session 2026-27, it brings me immense joy and pride to welcome you all to SGGS Khalsa College, Mahilpur. Whether you are returning to continue your scholarly journey or stepping into this vibrant campus for the first time, you are entering a space dedicated to excellence, innovation, and holistic development.

At SGGS Khalsa College, we believe in nurturing not just academically proficient individuals, but also responsible, empathetic, and forward-thinking citizens. Our commitment goes beyond traditional classroom teaching; we aim to foster curiosity, critical thinking, and the resilience needed to thrive in a dynamic global environment.

We strive tirelessly for strengthening our commitment to quality education by integrating updated technology into our pedagogy and expanding our extracurricular avenues. Our dedicated faculty members are mentors ready to ignite the flames of curiosity and guide you in discovering your latent talents.

I urge students to look beyond academic achievements and actively participate in sports, cultural activities, and community service. True education prepares you for life—instilling core values of integrity, discipline, and compassion. Remember, the choices you make when no one is watching define your character.

To the parents, thank you for trusting us with the future of your wards. We value your partnership and invite you to work together with us in shaping their journey.

I warmly welcome the new batch of 2026 and wish all my students a rewarding, productive, and memorable academic year. Believe in yourselves, aim high, and let us make this year a resounding success.

Dr. Parvinder Singh Principal, S.G.G.S. Khalsa College Mahilpur
