Frequently Asked Questions

Who can access the library?
All enrolled students, faculty members, and staff of S.G.G.S. Mahilpur have access.
Can I borrow e-books?
Yes — registered users can borrow e-books and access online journals through the digital library portal.
Are there study zones available?
Yes — quiet study zones and discussion areas are provided for students.
Do you provide research assistance?
Yes — library staff help with literature searches, citation management, and reference guidance.
