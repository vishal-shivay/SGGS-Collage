Why Choose M.A. Human Rights & Duties at S.G.G.S. Khalsa College Mahilpur?

M.A. Human Rights & Duties develops a deep understanding of human rights principles, legal frameworks, and civic responsibilities. Students engage in research, case studies, and advocacy projects for holistic learning.

  • Comprehensive understanding of human rights and duties
  • Analysis of legal frameworks and international conventions
  • Projects, case studies, and advocacy training
  • Preparation for careers in law, administration, and NGOs
Program Outcomes
  • Strong understanding of human rights principles and laws
  • Ability to analyze cases and develop advocacy strategies
  • Preparedness for careers in law, administration, NGOs, and policy-making
  • Research skills for higher studies and policy research
