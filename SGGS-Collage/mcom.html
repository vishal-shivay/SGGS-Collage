M.Com – Overview

M.Com is a 2-year postgraduate program (4 semesters) designed for students aspiring to excel in Accounting, Finance, Business Studies and Economics.

Why Choose M.Com?

  • In-depth understanding of accounting, finance, and business management
  • Opportunities for research projects, seminars, and case studies
  • Development of analytical, managerial, and decision-making skills
  • Prepares for higher studies like Ph.D., professional courses, or competitive exams

Career Opportunities

Graduates can work as Financial Analysts, Accountants, Tax Consultants, Auditors, Bank Managers, Academicians, or pursue further research and professional certifications like CA, CS, ICWA, CFA.

Admission

Admissions for M.Com are open! Eligible students (B.Com or equivalent with minimum 45% marks) can apply online and offline.

Course Duration & Eligibility

The M.Com program is a 2-year postgraduate course divided into 4 semesters.

Duration: 2 Years (4 Semesters)

Eligibility: B.Com or equivalent with minimum 45% marks

Program Outcomes
  • Mastery of accounting, finance, and business management principles
  • Hands-on experience with research, case studies, and projects
  • Ability to analyze financial data and provide strategic insights
  • Preparation for competitive exams and professional certifications
  • Enhanced managerial, communication, and leadership skills
