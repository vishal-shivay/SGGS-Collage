M.Sc. Instrumentation – Overview

The M.Sc. Instrumentation program is a 2-year postgraduate course (4 semesters) focused on advanced instrumentation, electronics, sensors, and analytical techniques. Students gain in-depth theoretical knowledge and practical lab experience.

Career Opportunities: Graduates can work as Instrumentation Engineers, Lab Analysts, Research Scientists, Electronics Specialists, or pursue higher studies like Ph.D.

Admission

Admissions for M.Sc. Instrumentation are open for students with a B.Sc. in Instrumentation, Physics, Electronics, or related fields can apply online or offline.
