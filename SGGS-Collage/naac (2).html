Re-Accredited with 'A' Grade — CGPA 3.21 (Cycle II)

S.G.G.S. Khalsa College Mahilpur has been re-accredited by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) with 'A' Grade. The institution continues to pursue quality enhancement through IQAC and has submitted the SSR for Cycle 3.

Grade A — CGPA 3.21
Peer Team Visited 2017
IQAC Established
SSR Cycle 3 Submitted

📌 About NAAC: The National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) is an autonomous body established by the UGC to assess and accredit institutions of higher education in India. Accreditation provides institutions a benchmark for quality and continual improvement across all academic, administrative and infrastructural domains.
