RE-ACCREDITED WITH 'A' GRADE

Score: 3.21 by NAAC  •  Cycle II  •  S.G.G.S. Khalsa College Mahilpur

S.G.G.S. Khalsa College Mahilpur has been Re-Accredited with 'A' Grade with a score of 3.21 by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) in Cycle II. This prestigious accreditation is a testament to the college's unwavering commitment to quality education, infrastructure, research, and student development since its establishment in 1946. The 'A' grade reflects the institution's excellence across all seven NAAC criteria.
