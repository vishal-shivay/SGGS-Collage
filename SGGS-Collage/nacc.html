NAAC Accreditation

National Assessment and Accreditation Council  •  Quality Excellence

S.G.G.S. Khalsa College Mahilpur is proud to be accredited by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC). The college is committed to maintaining and continuously improving the quality of education, infrastructure, and student support services. Our Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC) actively works to ensure that quality benchmarks are met across all academic and administrative dimensions. The college has submitted its Self Study Report (SSR) for Cycle 3, reflecting its ongoing journey of academic excellence and institutional growth since its establishment in 1946.

IQAC & Quality

Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC)

Planning  •  Monitoring  •  Enhancing Academic Quality

The Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC) at S.G.G.S. Khalsa College Mahilpur is the primary body responsible for channelizing the efforts and measures of an institution towards academic excellence. It works as a nodal agency of the institution for coordinating quality-related activities and ensures continuous improvement in the entire operations of the institution. The IQAC is committed to making quality enhancement a continuous process.

Quality Enhancement

Continuous efforts towards quality enhancement across academic programs, faculty development, student support, and institutional governance to meet NAAC benchmarks.

SSR Preparation

Meticulous preparation and submission of the Self Study Report (SSR) for NAAC Cycle 3, covering all seven criteria of assessment in comprehensive detail.

Stakeholder Feedback

Best Practices
