National Cadet Corps

National Cadet Corps (N.C.C.)

Youth Wing of Indian Armed Forces  •  Army, Navy & Air Force Wings

S.G.G.S. Khalsa College, Mahilpur has an N.C.C. unit which is a youth wing of Indian Armed Forces. N.C.C. helps to inculcate discipline, selfless service, leadership, truthfulness, self-sacrifice, and honesty among young cadet corps. N.C.C. is a platform for cadets to explore their interest in various domains like army, navy and air force wings.

Discipline & Leadership

NCC instills core values of discipline, leadership, and commitment in young cadets, preparing them for future challenges in both military and civilian life.

Selfless Service

Cadets learn the spirit of selfless service, truthfulness, self-sacrifice, and honesty — values that build character and contribute positively to society.

Army, Navy & Air Force

NCC provides a platform for cadets to explore their interest in the three wings — Army, Navy and Air Force — opening pathways to a career in the Indian Armed Forces.

National Service Scheme

National Service Scheme (N.S.S.)

Service Before Self  •  Community Outreach  •  Social Awareness

The S.G.G.S. Khalsa College has its N.S.S. unit which provides opportunity to the youth to understand the current problems of society. They learn the sense of Service before Self, the merit of leadership and discipline as well as feeling of involvement in national re-construction. They are involved in various activities like tree plantation, cleanliness drive, social awareness camps, and blood donation. Students interested in joining N.S.S. should contact the in-charge, N.S.S. Unit of the College to fill in the prescribed proforma at the time of admission.

Tree Plantation

NSS volunteers actively participate in tree plantation missions, contributing to environmental sustainability and green campus initiatives throughout the year.

Cleanliness Drive

Regular cleanliness drives are organized in and around the campus and local community areas, promoting hygiene awareness and environmental responsibility.

Social Awareness Camps

NSS unit conducts social awareness camps on topics like health, sanitation, COVID-19, and national issues — reaching out to communities through door-to-door campaigns.

Blood Donation

Blood donation drives are a regular NSS activity, encouraging students to contribute to life-saving medical needs and promoting a culture of humanitarian service.

Mask Distribution

NSS volunteers stitched and distributed masks during the COVID-19 pandemic, demonstrating hands-on community service and social responsibility in times of crisis.

Cyber Awareness

Special lectures and awareness programs on cyber safety and digital literacy are conducted by the NSS unit, keeping students and the community safe in the digital age.

4 July 2020  •  NSS, Youth Clubs & Red Ribbon Volunteers

ਡੋਰ ਟੂ ਡੋਰ ਕਰੋਨਾ ਜਾਗਰੁਕਤਾ ਮੁਹਿੰਮ — 4 ਜੁਲਾਈ 2020

30 ਜੂਨ ਨੂੰ ਮਾਣਯੋਗ ਰਾਣਾ ਗੁਰਮੀਤ ਸਿੰਘ ਮੰਤਰੀ ਖੇਡਾਂ ਤੇ ਯੁਵਕ ਸੇਵਾਵਾਂ, ਅਤੇ ਐਨ ਆਰ ਆਈ ਮਾਮਲੇ ਜੀ ਨਾਲ ਜੋ ਆਨਲਾਈਨ ਮੀਟਿੰਗ ਹੋਈ ਉਸ ਮੀਟਿੰਗ ਵਿੱਚ ਹੁਕਮ ਪ੍ਰਾਪਤ ਹੋਏ ਹਨ ਕਿ ਵਿਭਾਗ ਨਾਲ ਜੁੜੇ ਸਮੂਹ ਨੋਜਵਾਨ ਵਰਗ ਯੂਥ ਕਲੱਬਾਂ, ਐਨ ਐਸ ਐਸ ਵਲੰਟੀਅਰ, ਰੈਡ ਰੀਬਨ ਵਲੰਟੀਅਰ ਵੱਲੋਂ ਮਿਤੀ 4 ਜੁਲਾਈ 2020 ਨੂੰ ਡੋਰ ਟੂ ਡੋਰ ਕਰੋਨਾ ਜਾਗਰੁਕਤਾ ਮੁਹਿੰਮ ਚਲਾਈ ਜਾਵੇਗੀ।

ਸਮੂਹ ਯੂਥ ਕਲੱਬਾਂ, ਐਨ ਐਸ ਐਸ਼ ਪ੍ਰੋ ਅਫਸਰ ਅਤੇ ਰੈਡ ਰੀਬਨ ਪ੍ਰੋ ਅਫਸਰ ਇਸ ਸੰਬੰਧੀ ਆਪਣੀ ਤਿਆਰੀ ਕਰ ਲੈਣ ਜੀ ਆਪ ਜੀ ਵੱਲੋਂ ਖ਼ੁਦ ਵੀ ਮੁਹਿੰਮ ਨਾਲ ਜੁੜਿਆਂ ਜਾਵੇ। ਹਰ ਵਲੰਟੀਅਰ ਘੱਟ ਤੋਂ ਘੱਟ 20 ਘਰਾਂ ਵਿੱਚ ਜਾਗਰੂਕਤਾ ਫੈਲਾਵੇਂਗਾ ਅਤੇ ਹਰੇਕ ਸੰਸਥਾ / ਯੂਨਿਟ ਦੇ ਘੱਟ ਤੋਂ ਘੱਟ 50% ਵਲੰਟੀਅਰ ਜ਼ਰੂਰ ਸਾਮਿਲ ਹੋਣ। ਗਤੀਵਿਧੀ ਦੀਆਂ ਤਸਵੀਰਾਂ / ਵੀਡੀਓ ਜ਼ਰੂਰ ਬਣਾਈਆਂ ਜਾਣ ਅਤੇ ਵਿਭਾਗ ਨੂੰ ਭੇਜੀਆਂ ਜਾਣ। ਜਿਸ ਵੀ ਘਰ ਵਿੱਚ ਵਿਜਿਟ ਕਰੋ ਉਹਨਾਂ ਨੂੰ ਕੋਵਾ ਐਪ ਡਾਊਨਲੋਡ ਕਰਵਾਓ ਜੀ।

NSS Camps, Trips & Special Lectures

Anandpur Sahib Trip  •  Delhi 7-Day Camp  •  Cyber Awareness Day

The NSS Unit of S.G.G.S. Khalsa College organized a trip to Shri Anandpur Sahib for volunteers, enriching their cultural and spiritual experience. NSS students also represented the college at a 7-day camp held in Delhi, showcasing leadership and commitment at the national level. A Special Lecture on Cyber Awareness Day was also conducted by the NSS Unit to educate students about online safety and digital responsibility.

Shri Anandpur Sahib Trip

NSS volunteers visited Shri Anandpur Sahib — a spiritually and historically significant site — enriching their cultural understanding and sense of Sikh heritage and history.

Delhi 7-Day NSS Camp

Cyber Awareness Day Lecture

A special lecture on Cyber Awareness Day was conducted by the NSS Unit to educate students about online safety, digital literacy, and responsible use of technology.
