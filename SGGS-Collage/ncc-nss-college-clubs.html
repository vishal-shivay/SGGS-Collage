National Cadet Corps

National Cadet Corps (N.C.C.)

Youth Wing of Indian Armed Forces Army • Navy • Air Force Leadership & Discipline

S.G.G.S. Khalsa College, Mahilpur has an active N.C.C. unit which is a youth wing of the Indian Armed Forces. N.C.C. helps to inculcate discipline, selfless service, leadership, truthfulness, self-sacrifice, and honesty among young cadet corps. N.C.C. is a platform for cadets to explore their interest in various domains like the Army, Navy, and Air Force wings, preparing them for a life of national service and professional excellence.

Discipline & Leadership

Rigorous training instils discipline, punctuality, and leadership qualities essential for personal and professional life.

Armed Forces Pathway

NCC 'C' Certificate holders get direct entry advantages in SSB and Indian Armed Forces recruitment examinations.

Camp Training

Annual Training Camps, Combined Annual Training Camps (CATC), and Republic Day Parade selection opportunities.

Community Service

Regular community service activities, disaster relief participation, and social welfare programmes across the region.

National Service Scheme

National Service Scheme (N.S.S.)

Service Before Self Community Development Environmental Activities

The S.G.G.S. Khalsa College has its N.S.S. unit which provides opportunity to the youth to understand the current problems of society. They learn the sense of Service before Self, the merit of leadership and discipline as well as a feeling of involvement in national re-construction. They are involved in various activities like tree plantation, cleanliness drives, social awareness camps, and blood donation. Students interested in joining N.S.S. should contact the incharge, N.S.S. Unit of the College to fill in the prescribed proforma at the time of admission.

Tree Plantation

Regular plantation drives across the campus and surrounding areas to promote environmental awareness and green cover.

Cleanliness Drive

Swachh Bharat-inspired cleanliness campaigns in the college campus, villages, and public spaces of Mahilpur.

Blood Donation

Organising blood donation camps in collaboration with civil hospitals, encouraging students to donate blood voluntarily.

Social Awareness

Awareness camps on health, hygiene, literacy, gender equality, and social issues to empower local communities.

Cyber Awareness

Special lectures and awareness drives educating students and community members about cyber safety and digital literacy.

Educational Trips

Spiritual and educational excursions including the Anandpur Sahib visit and participation in national-level NSS camps.

Door-to-Door Corona Awareness Mission — 4 July 2020
Official NSS Notice (Punjabi)

30 ਜੂਨ ਨੂੰ ਮਾਣਯੋਗ ਰਾਣਾ ਗੁਰਮੀਤ ਸਿੰਘ ਮੰਤਰੀ ਖੇਡਾਂ ਤੇ ਯੁਵਕ ਸੇਵਾਵਾ, ਅਤੇ ਐਨ ਆਰ ਆਈ ਮਾਮਲੇ ਜੀ ਨਾਲ ਜੋ ਆਨਲਾਈਨ ਮੀਟਿੰਗ ਹੋਈ ਉਸ ਮੀਟਿੰਗ ਵਿੱਚ ਹੁਕਮ ਪ੍ਰਾਪਤ ਹੋਏ ਹਨ ਕਿ ਵਿਭਾਗ ਨਾਲ ਜੁੜੇ ਸਮੂਹ ਨੋਜਵਾਨ ਵਰਗ ਯੂਥ ਕਲੱਬਾਂ, ਐਨ ਐਸ ਐਸ ਵਲੰਟੀਅਰ, ਰੈਡ ਰੀਬਨ ਵਲੰਟੀਅਰ ਵੱਲੋਂ ਮਿਤੀ 4 ਜੁਲਾਈ 2020 ਨੂੰ ਡੋਰ ਟੂ ਡੋਰ ਕਰੋਨਾ ਜਾਗਰੁਕਤਾ ਮੁਹਿੰਮ ਚਲਾਈ ਜਾਵੇਗੀ। ਹਰ ਵਲੰਟੀਅਰ ਘੱਟ ਤੋਂ ਘੱਟ 20 ਘਰਾਂ ਵਿੱਚ ਜਾਗਰੂਕਤਾ ਫੈਲਾਵੇਗਾ।

On 4th July 2020, S.G.G.S. Khalsa College NSS volunteers, Red Ribbon volunteers, and Youth Club members conducted a mass Door-to-Door Corona Awareness Campaign across Mahilpur town and surrounding villages. Each volunteer visited a minimum of 20 households spreading awareness about COVID-19 safety protocols and helping people install the COVA app.

Digital Literacy
Special Lecture on Cyber Awareness Day — NSS Unit

The NSS Unit of S.G.G.S. Khalsa College Mahilpur conducted a Special Lecture on Cyber Awareness Day to educate students about online safety, cyber fraud prevention, social media responsibility, and secure digital practices. Expert speakers were invited to guide students on navigating the digital world safely and responsibly.

Online Safety

Awareness about phishing, cyber fraud, and safe browsing practices for students and their families.

Social Media Responsibility

Guidance on responsible social media usage, privacy settings, and the legal implications of online behaviour.

Digital Identity Protection

Sessions on protecting personal data, password hygiene, and recognising suspicious online activities.

Educational Excursion
Shri Anandpur Sahib Trip — NSS Unit (May 2022)

The NSS Unit of S.G.G.S. Khalsa College organised an enriching educational and spiritual excursion to Shri Anandpur Sahib in May 2022. Students visited the sacred Sikh heritage site, connecting with Punjabi history, culture, and the teachings of Guru Gobind Singh Ji, reinforcing values of courage, service, and community.
