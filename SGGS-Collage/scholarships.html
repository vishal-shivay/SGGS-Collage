Government Scholarship

Post-Matric Scholarship Scheme for Scheduled Castes

The objective of the scheme is to provide financial assistance to the Scheduled Caste students studying at Post-Matric or Post-Secondary stage to enable them to complete their education. These scholarships are paid to students whose parents'/guardians' income from all sources does not exceed Rs. 2,50,000/- per annum.

Eligibility

Students belonging to Scheduled Caste category studying at Post-Matric/Post-Secondary stage.

Income Limit

Parents'/guardians' income from all sources must not exceed Rs. 2,50,000/- per annum.

Online Application

Students must apply online before the due date notified by the college.

Government Scholarship

Post-Matric Scholarship Scheme for OBCs

Other Backward Classes

The objective of the scheme is to provide financial assistance to the OBC students studying at Post-Matric or Post-Secondary stage to enable them to complete their education.

Employed Students

Whose income combined with income of their parents/guardians does not exceed Rs. 1 lac per annum.

Unemployed Students

Whose parents'/guardians' income does not exceed Rs. 1 lac per annum.

Online Application

Government Scholarship

Post-Matric Scholarship for Minority Communities

The objective is to award scholarships to meritorious students belonging to economically weaker sections of minority communities from Class XI to Ph.D. so as to provide better opportunities for higher education, increase their rate of attainment in higher education, and enhance their employability.

Eligible Communities

Muslim / Christian / Sikh / Buddhist / Parsi communities.

Marks Required

Not less than 50% marks in the previous final examination.

Income Limit

Annual income of parents/guardians from all sources does not exceed Rs. 2 lac.

Level of Study

Class XI to Ph.D. — covering the full spectrum of higher education.

Sarbat Da Bhala Charitable Trust (SDBCT)

Sunny Oberoi Scholarships Scheme

For intelligent and hard working students from underprivileged sections

SARBAT DA BHALA CHARITABLE TRUST (SDBCT) is a philanthropic body dedicated to the cause of extending economic benefits to the underprivileged and hitherto neglected sections of society. The Trust grants financial assistance under the Sunny Oberoi Scholarships Scheme where intelligent and hard-working students are helped to pursue their higher education.

Eligibility Categories

Economically Weaker

Students belonging to economically weaker sections of society.

Lost Father (Earning Member)

A student who has lost his/her father — the earning member of the family.

Divorced Mother

Students whose mother is a divorcee and not getting any supporting allowance from the husband.

Natural Disaster

Students whose families are affected by natural disaster.

Differently Abled

Students who are differently abled.

Trust Recommended

Students recommended by the Managing Trustee of Sarbat Da Bhala Charitable Trust (Regd.).

Selection Rules

  • 1Students whose parents' annual income is less than one lakh rupees.
  • 2Student must fulfil the mandatory class attendance conditions.
  • 3Student must not be getting scholarship/stipend on the basis of SC/BC category or Sikh Minority community Scholarship Scheme. They must first approach concerned authorities before applying to the Trust.
  • 4Students must have more than 65% marks in the qualifying examination.
