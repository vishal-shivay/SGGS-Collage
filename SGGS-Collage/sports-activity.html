Sports Activities in College

Sports Activities in College

Football Nursery of Asia  •  Arjuna Awardees  •  PU Champions

The College has occupied a noteworthy place in the sports history of India and is popularly known as a 'FOOTBALL NURSERY OF ASIA'. Footballer Jarnail Singh (Arjuna Awardee) and Gurdev Singh Gill (Arjuna Awardee) are the glorious production of S.G.G.S. Khalsa College Mahilpur. The College possesses well-maintained sports grounds and facilities for games such as football, hockey, cricket, basketball, volleyball, athletics and various indoor games. The College also has an ultra-modern gymnasium and a hall for indoor games. It is one of the very few colleges in India to possess its own fabric. It is a matter of great honour that the Football team (Men) won the Panjab University A Division Football Championship for the last 20 years regularly and the Women team also won the Football Championship of the Panjab University.

Football — Nursery of Asia

Popularly known as the 'Football Nursery of Asia', the college produced legendary Arjuna Awardees Jarnail Singh and Gurdev Singh Gill. Men's team won PU A Division Championship 20+ years consecutively.

Women Football Champions

The Women Football team also won the Panjab University Football Championship, reflecting the college's dominance in football at both men's and women's levels.

Ultra-Modern Gymnasium

The college has an ultra-modern gymnasium and a dedicated indoor games hall, providing students with professional-grade fitness and sports training facilities year-round.

Athletics & Track

Well-maintained athletics tracks and grounds support cross-country running, intramural athletics meets, and inter-college competitions for both boys and girls.

Multi-Sport Grounds

Dedicated grounds for hockey, cricket, basketball, volleyball, kabaddi, and other games — ensuring every student has access to diverse sports opportunities throughout the year.

PU Championships

S.G.G.S. Khalsa College regularly stands among the top colleges in Panjab University sports. The college stood 2nd overall in PU Championship 2016-17 and consistently wins medals across disciplines.

Panjab University Gymnastics 2016-17

PU Gymnastics Inter-College Tournament 2016-17

18 Medals Total  •  Gold-3, Silver-10, Bronze-5  •  2nd Overall  •  Best Gymnast: Gagan

S.G.G.S. Khalsa College Mahilpur won total 18 medals (Gold-3, Silver-10, and Bronze-5) in Panjab University Gymnastics Inter College Tournament 2016-2017. Our college student Gagan was declared Best Gymnast of the Tournament. Overall S.G.G.S. Khalsa College Mahilpur stood Second in Panjab University Championship 2016-2017.

3 Gold Medals

S.G.G.S. Khalsa College won 3 prestigious Gold medals in the Panjab University Gymnastics Inter-College Tournament 2016-17, demonstrating elite-level performance.

10 Silver Medals

10 Silver medals were secured across various gymnastics events, reflecting the consistent and competitive performance of our gymnastics team throughout the tournament.

5 Bronze Medals

5 Bronze medals completed the college's remarkable total of 18 medals — one of the strongest performances by any single institution in the Panjab University gymnastics championship.

Best Gymnast — Gagan

Student Gagan of S.G.G.S. Khalsa College Mahilpur was declared the Best Gymnast of the Tournament — a remarkable individual achievement at the university level championship.

2nd Overall — PU Championship

S.G.G.S. Khalsa College Mahilpur stood Second Overall in the Panjab University Championship 2016-17, a remarkable collective achievement for the entire institution.

PU Prize Distribution — 30 March 2016

Panjab University Annual Prize Distribution Function held on 30-03-2016 by Directorate of Sports PU Chandigarh. Football team awarded Trophy for 1st position. Six players honoured ₹21,000/- each for representing PU in All India Inter University Football Championship (1st Position).

Football & Campus Sports

Football Tournament & Campus Sports Activities

Extramural & Intramural Tournaments  •  Cross Country  •  Volleyball  •  Kabaddi  •  Basketball

Football has been at the heart of S.G.G.S. Khalsa College since its inception. The college organizes extramural and intramural football tournaments every year. Alongside football, the campus hosts regular intramural competitions in cross-country running, volleyball, kabaddi, basketball, athletics and many more sports. The college grounds are meticulously maintained for all sporting events, and students also observe moments of respect for national martyrs as part of their sportsmanship values.
